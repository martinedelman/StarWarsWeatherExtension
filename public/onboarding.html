FAQ

How do I change the temperature unit / language?

To access all available settings, click on the puzzle piece icon located in the top right corner of your Chrome/Firefox browser and choose "Star Wars Weather" from the options. This will open a dropdown menu from which you can make your desired changes.


How often does the extension update the weather information?

The extension will get the most accurate weather information when you open a new tab. It will keep that information stored until the end of the hour. For example, if you open a new tab at 8:37, the extension will keep the weather information stored until 9:00. This is done to make sure the information is accurate and to reduce the time it takes to load the information again.

How do I turn on location services?

  1. Click on the three dots (More) icon located in the top right-hand corner of the browser window.
  2. Click on "Settings" from the dropdown menu.
  3. On The right side, click "privacy and security"
  4. Click on "Site settings".
  5. Scroll down and click on "Location".
  6. Click "Sites can ask for your location" to turn it on.

IMPORTANT: If you are using a Mac, please follow these sets of instructions also:

  1. Click on the Apple menu icon located in the top left-hand corner of your screen.
  2. Click on "System Preferences" from the dropdown menu.
  3. Click on "Security & Privacy".
  4. Click on the "Privacy" tab.
  5. Scroll down and click on "Location Services".
  6. If the padlock icon in the bottom left-hand corner of the window is locked, click on it and enter your administrator password to unlock it.
  7. Scroll down and find "Google Chrome" in the list of apps.
  8. Make sure the checkbox next to "Google Chrome" is checked.
